Project Overview

"Dimension Puzzle" is an innovative co-op puzzle game developed in Unreal Engine 5. Two players must work together to solve intricate puzzles across different dimensions, each with its unique properties. One player navigates the "real" world while the other explores parallel dimensions such as a mirror world, a time-altered realm, or the past.

As the lead developer, I leveraged Unreal Engine 5's powerful features and C++ to create a seamless multiplayer experience with dimension-switching mechanics and challenging puzzles.

Project Details

  • Engine: Unreal Engine 5 (C++)
  • Team Size: 1 (Programmer, Designer)
  • Role: Lead Programmer
  • Development Time: 3 Months

Key Features

  • Dimension Switching Mechanic: Players can switch between different dimensions, each affecting puzzle-solving strategies.
  • Co-op Gameplay: Two-player cooperative gameplay requiring communication and coordination.
  • Custom Interaction System: Allows players to interact with various objects across dimensions.
  • Unique Puzzles: Time-based, mirror-world, and interdimensional interaction puzzles.
  • Atmospheric Design: Distinct visual styles for each dimension to enhance immersion.

Lessons Learned and Challenges Faced

Lessons Learned:

  • Multiplayer Synchronization: Implementing robust netcode for seamless co-op experiences.
  • Dimension Management: Creating a flexible system to handle different world states efficiently.
  • Puzzle Design: Balancing difficulty and cooperation requirements in multi-dimensional puzzles.
  • Performance Optimization: Efficiently managing resources across multiple dimensions.

Challenges Faced:

  • Networking Complexities: Ensuring smooth gameplay across different network conditions.
  • Dimension Transitions: Creating seamless and visually appealing transitions between dimensions.
  • Puzzle Complexity: Designing puzzles that require cooperation without being frustratingly difficult.
  • Player Coordination: Developing clear communication systems for players in different dimensions.
  • Game Balance: Ensuring that both players feel equally engaged and important in puzzle-solving.
